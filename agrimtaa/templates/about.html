Welcome to Agri Mtaa

Founded in 2024, Agri Mtaa is on a mission to redefine agriculture in Kenya and beyond. By seamlessly integrating tradition and technology, we empower farmers and connect them with global markets, unlocking a future of limitless possibilities.

Our Journey

Agri Mtaa began as a small initiative to address the struggles faced by farmers in accessing markets, tools, and resources. Armed with a vision of transforming agriculture, we grew into a robust platform that caters to farmers, buyers, renters, and service providers.

Over the years, we have expanded our offerings to include transport services, weather insights, and expert recommendations. By fostering collaboration, we ensure every farmer thrives in their endeavors.
